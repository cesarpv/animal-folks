Resources

There is photo and video documentation of puppy mills throughout the United States, as well as documentation of dog auctions and pet store conditions where puppies are bought and sold. Many commercial breeding facilities, however, are hidden from view. As kennels are private property, consumers are often not allowed on-site to assess conditions. The inhumanity within the breeding industry is not just due to problematic breeding conditions. It is also due to the fact that "mass" breeding creates unwanted dogs and cats who, if abandoned later and are not adopted, may be euthanized by pounds or shelters. Please view video below.
